HOTEL IN KATHMANDU

There are thousands of hotel and guest house in Kathmandu valley. Especially near Bus Park, there are a lot of guest houses. Waling Fulbari is the one, which is also close from BG Mall and New Bus Park, Kathmandu. Waling Fulbari Guest House is not far from airport. This guest house is best for you if you are coming from abroad as Nepal biggest bus station/park is close from this guest house and you can directly aboard in bus and go any corner of Nepal. If you need the guest house, Waling Fulbari Guest House is affrodable guest house with attached bathroom in each room, which also provide room service, and other services such as airport pickup and drop, food and lodging.
