HOTEL IN NEW BUSPARK, GONGABU

KATHMANDU

WALING FULBARI GUEST HOUSE

Waling Fulbari hotel is owned by Am Bahadur Kunwar who was born in Hugdi of Syangja district and his parents are in Waling. From Waling Bazar, the owner is motivated and named the Guest House as Waling Fulbari Guest House. The hotel has been providing services since two decades in the central area of Gongabu, New Bus Park Area, Kathmandu, Nepal. The hotel took a building in rent previously but now it has its own new building. The hotel is nearly 200 meters inside from BG Mall, which is just close to the entrance of New Bus Park. The hotel has a quiet and nice environment.

The hotel provides airport pickup and drops services as well as food and lodging facilities to our guest. The guest house has 22 rooms with attached bathroom on each at a reasonable price. It also provides the necessary information and support to the guests who are going to abroad for the job or other purposes.
